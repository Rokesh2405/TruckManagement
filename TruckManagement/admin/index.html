Shree Kalikamba Southarda Sahakari Niyamitha

Welcome to Shree Kalikamba Southarda Sahakari Niyamitha, a growing Sahakari in the coastal area of Karnataka state which strives for the financial upliftment of the members. A product by the young Co-Operators, the Co-Operative started its functioning in 2001. The Co-Operative is registered under Karnataka State Souharda Sahakari Act 1997; its Head Office is situated in yeshwanthpur, Bangalore. A due respect to the Souharda Co-Operative Principles has made the Co-Operative to focus on continued growth.
